My operating model

  • Start with a real user constraint: rules, time, data limits, or accessibility needs.
  • Build fast prototypes: the simplest thing that can be tested.
  • Validate with evidence: CFD comparisons, model evaluation, or user feedback.
  • Ship and iterate: improve reliability and adoption, not just a demo.

What I want to do

I want to deepen my engineering foundation while learning how to scale technology into products through the Wharton lens. I’m especially interested in building AI systems that are reliable enough for real users in high-stakes contexts (accessibility and education), and in learning how to evaluate product-market fit, distribution, and responsible deployment.

  • Near-term goal: extend SignifAI from prototype → robust product (evaluation, data strategy, user testing).
  • Long-term goal: build tools that reduce communication barriers across languages and modalities.
